About Me

Welcome! I am an active scientific researcher and academic focusing on next-generation semiconductor materials, wide-bandgap microelectronic frameworks, and micro-electromechanical systems (MEMS).

My work bridges underlying materials science exploration with practical device architecture fabrication. This includes designing scalable hardware prototypes, establishing robust engineering criteria for optoelectronic devices, and translating research configurations into clean physical components capable of precise metrics deployment.
